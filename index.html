CarlHacks

Northfield, MN
April 8-10, 2016

Register Now

200 students. 35 hours. Limitless possibilities.

Carleton College is pleased to announce its second large-scale hackathon. Whether you've been programming for five minutes or five years, you'll find CarlHacks is an incredible space to learn dramatically more. Code your next big idea for 35 hours through day and night (napping strongly encouraged), attend a cornucopia of workshops, and get inspired by an awesome, inclusive community of hackers.

Why CarlHacks?

  • Compete in teams to win exciting prizes (last year, the members of the winning team each took home a drone)

  • Get one-on-one help from professors and professional engineers

  • Attend Git and web development classes for beginners, and workshops on advanced topics throughout the event

  • Chat with companies and kickstart your internship or job search

  • Hack with Oculus Rifts, Pebble watches, Leap Motions, and more from the hardware lab

  • Eat delicious catered meals, snacks, and of course, coffee

  • Need a coding break? Take a nap on an air mattress, a shower in our bathrooms, walk around our 880-acre arboretum, or participate in one of our exciting mini-events, complete with mini-prizes

Any undergraduate enrolled in an North American college during the 2014–2015 academic year is eligible to participate. Interested? Read on for more details.

We'll post a more detailed schedule in late February.

Packing List

CarlHacks is completely free to attend. You'll need a laptop, charger, and student ID. We'd also recommend bringing toiletries, shower shoes, a change of clothing, and a pillow. We'll provide meals, snacks, showers, and air beds for napping. If you don't have a project idea or team, don't worry! There will be team-forming events, and we find some of our best ideas come during the hackathon, not before. If you come with a team, note that the maximum team size is four.

Traveling to CarlHacks

CarlHacks is hosted in Northfield, Minnesota, at Carleton College's Recreation Center. If you're not already in Northfield, there are plenty of ways to get here.

  • By Bus — We have buses picking up hackers from schools near the Twin Cities.

  • By Plane — We have a limited number of $150 airfare reimbursements for visitors from other states. A bus will pick you up from the airport. If you need one, be sure to apply before February 27th.

  • By Car — You're welcome to drive over! We're located at 501 Three Oaks Drive, Northfield, MN.

If you have any concerns or questions about travel, we're here to help! Shoot us an email at team@carlhacks.com.
